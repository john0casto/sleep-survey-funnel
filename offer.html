Frequently asked questions

How does the program work? ▾
After completing your assessment and selecting a plan, a board-certified physician reviews your information. Once approved, your personalized sleep medication is prescribed and shipped directly to your door. You'll have ongoing access to your care team for support.
What medications are prescribed? ▾
Our physicians prescribe FDA-approved sleep medications tailored to your specific needs. The exact medication is determined after your physician review based on your medical history, sleep patterns, and other health factors discussed in your assessment.
Do I need insurance? ▾
No insurance is required. The cost of your consultation, prescription, and medication are all included in your monthly plan. There are no hidden fees.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes. There are no contracts or long-term commitments. You can cancel at any time without needing to speak with anyone — simply log in to your account or contact our support team.
How quickly will I see results? ▾
Most patients report improved sleep within the first week of starting their medication. Full benefits are typically experienced within 2-4 weeks as your body adjusts to a healthier sleep pattern.
Is my information secure? ▾
Absolutely. All personal and medical information is protected under HIPAA regulations and encrypted with 256-bit SSL. Your data is never shared with third parties.
